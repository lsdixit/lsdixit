### 🦀 My professional background! 
I come from a marine science background (with a special love of invertebrates 🦐), graduating University of California, Davis in 2018 with a Bachelors of Science in Evolution, Ecology, and Biodiversity. Since then, I have worked in a variety of fields from outdoor education, microbiology, toxicology, field work, CA state monitoring, and marine conservation. Over time, my interest in data science grew and eventually led me to my Masters at UCSB Bren school! While at my masters, I have developed skills in data communication and visualization, new languages such as R, Python, and SQL, and new analytical techniques like statistical modeling and machine learning.
